A message from Fr. Seraphim Dedes

Dear DCS users:
Thank you.

Yes, thanks to you and your generous support over the past year, AGES Initiatives was able to maintain its public service of providing liturgical texts and music online for use in the Orthodox Church of Christ, for the glory of God. Not only were we able to maintain our offerings, but we added several enhancements and new functionalities as well.

This is the time of year that we make our appeal for your continued financial support. We invite you to support AGES Initiatives financially. We provide a free service (or services, as the case may be), that you rely on and use on a regular basis. Your financial support is needed to maintain and expand our programs to provide more and better liturgical texts and music.
